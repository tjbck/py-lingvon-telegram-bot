What's the definition of figment?
a thing that someone believes to be real but that exists only in their imagination.
For example: "it really was Ross and not a figment of her overheated imagination"
How is invention similar?
The word invention means the action of inventing something, typically a process or device.
For example: "the invention of printing in the 15th century"

The word insinuation means an unpleasant hint or suggestion of something bad.
For example: "I've done nothing to deserve all your vicious insinuations"
